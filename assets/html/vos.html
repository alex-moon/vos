About VOS

navigate_before Back

VOS started life as a side project, for fun, driven by a desire to do a walk of the planets, starting at the sun, with the planets to scale relative to the sun being one meter wide.

Having researched the respective sizes and distances, Alex was inclined to wonder what a second walk, scaled down 10,000 times, would look like; and how many such walks one would do before hitting the end of the observable universe.

Alex found calculating these walks by hand tedious and prone to human error, so, naturally, he did what any software engineer in his position would: he wrote an app.
